ABOUT SCHOLAR-SHIPS.com

The cost of tuition is rising for students across the country and college graduates are graduating with more student loan debt than ever before. Scholar-ships.com introduces an engageent platform where friends, family, and sponsors can pledge a donation, wheather monetary or material, to help the student afford the cost of higher education.

Our platform is unique to others because donors can either just send a donation or list service opportunties that students can complete in order to receive a cash match into the student's college fund. Need a math tutor, house sitter, or tech whiz? Know that your money is going towards brightening these young adults' futures.
